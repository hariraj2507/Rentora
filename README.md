🏠 Rentora – AI-Powered Peer-to-Peer Rental Platform for Students
📌 Overview

Rentora is an AI-powered, campus-focused peer-to-peer rental platform designed to help students rent and lend items within their community.

Students often purchase items like books, calculators, electronics, or bikes that are used only temporarily. These items remain idle after use, while other students still need them.

Rentora bridges this gap by enabling safe, cost-effective, and sustainable resource sharing.

🎯 Problem Statement
Students buy items for short-term use
Items remain unused after usage
Other students cannot afford to buy them
Existing platforms lack:
Trust
Campus-specific focus
Rental-based workflow
💡 Solution

Rentora provides a trusted rental ecosystem where:

Students can list items for rent
Other students can book them for specific durations
Transactions are secured with trust and verification systems
